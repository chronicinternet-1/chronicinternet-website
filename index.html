About Chronic Internet

Chronic Internet is a digital media and products company founded by Steve Ysreal Monas. We build, operate, and monetize digital platforms across news media, business tools, consumer apps, and publishing.

Our business model spans multiple revenue streams: digital product sales, subscription media, display advertising, affiliate marketing, and mobile app revenue. We sell digital goods and services directly to consumers and businesses through our portfolio of websites and applications.
