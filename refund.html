30-Day Money-Back Guarantee. If you're not satisfied with any digital product purchase, we'll refund you — no questions asked — within 30 days of purchase.

All digital products sold through Bevoza.com and our other platforms come with a 30-day money-back guarantee. If the product doesn't meet your expectations, simply email us and we'll process a full refund within 5-7 business days.

Subscriptions

Subscription services (such as NewsAnarchist Premium) can be canceled at any time from your account settings or by contacting us. Upon cancellation:

  • You retain access through the end of your current billing period

  • Refunds for partial periods are available within the first 30 days

How to Request a Refund

Email zeno@chronicinternet.com with:

  • Your name and email used for purchase
  • The product or subscription you'd like refunded

We typically process refunds within 2 business days. Funds may take 5-10 business days to appear on your statement depending on your bank.

Exceptions

Refunds are not available for:
